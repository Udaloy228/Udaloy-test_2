Browse Deals

Browse our partners for brands you'd like to promote. We exclusively invite companies that are environmentally and socially conscious, so you can be proud to market for any of them.

Get A Promo Code

Once you have chosen a brand, click 'Get promo code' to receive a personal promo code connected just to you.

Spread The Word

Tell your friends and followers about the company you have chosen to promote. Don't forget to include your promo code!

Earn Income

For every sale that is linked to your promo code, you will receive a commission. Commissions vary by brand and product, but can be up to 20% of the sale price.

Grow Your Network

Use your username as an invite code to sign up your friends and followers as affiliates. Growing your network not only helps those you sign up make money, it also makes you money.

Earn Passive Income

Everyone who is signed up below you, whether you invited them or someone else in your chain invited them, earns passive income for you. You receive a percentage of their commission.

The Model

When you receive a commission from a sale of someone in your network, your commission comes out of the profits of Infinite Affiliates, not the affiliate who sold the product. Don't worry your friends will make just as much money.

How do I invite my friends?
Your username is your invite code! So friends can either enter your code manually upon signup, or you can share your invite link which can be found in the settings page of the app.
